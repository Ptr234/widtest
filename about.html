About
Women in Design Uganda

Women in Design Uganda was created to close the visibility and access gap for female professionals in the design industry. Despite their talent and drive, many women in architecture, interior design, graphic design, and related fields remain underrepresented in projects, partnerships, and leadership.

By spotlighting local talent and building a trusted design ecosystem, we're changing the narrative, one collaboration at a time.
